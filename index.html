Veteran-Aligned Funding

From Combat to Community

Shawn Livingston's journey — combat veteran, heroin addiction survivor, ultra runner, founder — is one of the most compelling grant narratives in the recovery space. These funders specifically prioritize veteran-led or veteran-serving organizations.

"Sobriety didn't just save Shawn's life — it reshaped it. Today he channels the same mental resilience that carried him through recovery into everything he does." — I Am Redemption
Why the Veteran Angle Unlocks More Funding
Unique Founder Story
Combat veteran + addiction + recovery + 100-mile ultra run. This narrative exceeds any standard nonprofit story funders read. Document it quantitatively.
Dual Eligibility
IAR qualifies for both veteran-specific grants AND general addiction/reentry grants — doubling the addressable funding pool compared to single-focus nonprofits.
Corporate Priority
Wells Fargo, Bank of America, USAA, and many Fortune 500 companies have explicit veteran giving mandates. Military service is often the fastest path to corporate grant relationships.
